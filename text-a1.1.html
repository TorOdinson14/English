Training Text for English Learners - Level A1

My name is Sarah. I live in a small town near the mountains. Every day, I wake up early and go for a walk with my dog, Max. We like to walk around the park and look at the trees and flowers. After our walk, I have breakfast. I usually eat toast and drink tea. Then, I go to work. I work at a bookstore in the town center. I help people find books and sometimes read to children. In the afternoon, I go home and make lunch. Sometimes, I make sandwiches, and other times, I make a salad. After lunch, I like to read books or watch a movie. I also like to paint pictures of the mountains and the trees. In the evening, I cook dinner. I often make pasta or rice with vegetables. After dinner, I sit in my garden and listen to the birds. Then, I go to bed and get ready for another day.
