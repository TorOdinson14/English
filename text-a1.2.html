Daily Routine

I wake up at 7 o'clock in the morning. I brush my teeth and take a shower. Then I have breakfast. I eat toast and drink coffee. After breakfast, I go to work. I work in an office. I have lunch at 12 o'clock. I eat a sandwich and drink water. In the afternoon, I have meetings. I finish work at 5 o'clock. I go home and cook dinner. I eat dinner with my family. After dinner, I watch TV or read a book. I go to bed at 10 o'clock.
